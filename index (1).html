Our story

The Native Narrative LLC is an Indigenous‑led social enterprise rooted in Minneapolis that designs place‑based tours, cultural programs, and narrative‑change resources. Our companion platform, Visit Mnisota, uplifts authentic stories from Minnesota’s 11 Tribal Nations and Native communities statewide.

We collaborate with nonprofits, schools, and civic partners to cultivate understanding and belonging through education, art, and experience.

  • American Indian Cultural Corridor walk on Franklin Ave
  • Custom itineraries & youth scavenger hunts
  • Trainings on respectful engagement & representation
